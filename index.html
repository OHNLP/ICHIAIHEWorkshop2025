About the Workshop

The Bridging the Digital Divide: 1st AIM-AHEAD Workshop for AI for Health Equity (AIHE) aims to bring together researchers, practitioners, and community members to:

  • Explore challenges and opportunities of using AI/ML to promote health equity
  • Showcase latest research on digital divide issues in healthcare
  • Foster collaborations between diverse institutions, communities, and stakeholders

We welcome submissions (papers, talks, demos) from all disciplines and backgrounds— especially those historically under-resourced—to advance AI in health equity.

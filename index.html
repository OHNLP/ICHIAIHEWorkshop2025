June 18-21, 2025 in Rende, Italy

In conjunction with IEEE ICHI 2025

About

The Bridging the Digital Divide: The 1st Workshop for AI in Advancing Health aims to bring together researchers, practitioners, and community members to:

  • Explore challenges and opportunities of using AI/ML to promote healthy communities
  • Showcase latest research on digital divide issues in healthcare
  • Foster collaborations between various communities and stakeholders

Submission

We are inviting contributions to the AIHE 2025, including regular papers, short papers, and abstracts for posters:

  • Regular papers (8-10 pages, references not counted): describes mature ideas with substantial implementation/data analysis.
  • Short papers (4-6 pages, excluding references): describes innovative ideas with preliminary validation.
  • Abstracts (1 page, including references): describes work in progress or preliminary results.

Your submissions must adhere to the IEEE Proceedings Format . All papers will be submitted via EasyChair . When choosing a Track, please select “ICHI - AIHE workshop”.

Topics for submission may include any aspect of using AI to advance health. Potential areas of focus include:

  • AI/ML efforts and applications for promoting healthy communities
  • Methodological and research design innovations to ensure valid and generalizable AI/ML discovery
  • Ethical considerations and the role of AI/ML in addressing the digital divide
  • Approaches for improving data quality and model reliability
  • Reproducibility, transparency, and responsible processes in AI/ML development for health

Submissions must contain author names & affiliations. The review process will be single-blinded.

We welcome submissions (papers, talks, demos) from all disciplines and backgrounds— especially those historically under-resourced—to advance AI in promoting healthy communities.
